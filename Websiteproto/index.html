overview

The expansion of the Kinder Morgan Pipeline is located in British Columbia, generally in the Greater Vancouver region. The environmental risk of oil spills due to pipeline production will increase, because more ships will be moving through the BC waters. Kinder Morgan is one of the largest energy infrastructure companies in North America. It owns an interest in or operate approximately 84,000 miles of pipelines and 155 terminals. The pipelines transport natural gas, gasoline, crude oil, carbon dioxide (CO2) and more. Our terminals store and handle petroleum products, chemicals and other products.
